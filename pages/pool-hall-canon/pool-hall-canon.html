  • Pool Hall Canon
  • for pool hall and open instrumentation (2020)

Music for the pool hall.. The game provides rhythmic structure; instrumentalists elobarate on it with a simple melody.

The piece is written as a six-voiced canon, which works in a typical hall dedicated to billiards games, but it can be adapted to pool halls or bars with fewer than six tables.
