  • 10 cards
  • for one, two or three people (2020)

1. Each card is 30" long.
2. Cards are read from top to bottom.
3. Pen marks indicate durations proportional to the size of the card.
4. Color denotes timbre and character with respect to both positive sounds and silences.
5. The number in the bottom right corner indicates the length of silence following that card, in seconds.
6. Dynamics are pp to mf.
7. Each person shuffles their deck before playing.

Note: Each deck consists of the same ten cards, but the length of silence between each card was randomly decided. In each deck, the gaps between cards sum to 5', so that the total length of the piece is 10'. The notation requires instruments capable of sustained sounds, but the instrumentation is open.
